GMS - Game Management System Login Intructions

1. If you already have an account (username and password), enter them into the Login Page to proceed to the Home Page. If not, continue to step 2.

2. At the Login Page, press the "Register Here!" button

3. Enter your Desired Username and Desired Passwords into the appropriate text fields. Then press the Sign Up button.

4. You will be redirected to the Login Page where you will enter your Username and Password. Then you will be redirected to the Home Page.
